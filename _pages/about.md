I completed my PhD at the University of Cambridge in 2023 within the <a href='https://www.maths.cam.ac.uk/postgrad/cca/research-cambridge-centre-analysis'>Cambridge Centre for Analysis</a> and the Cambridge Image Analysis Group. I received a fully funded studetship by <a href='https://www.ccimi.maths.cam.ac.uk/'> Cantab Capital Institute for the Mathematics of Information</a> and was fortunate to be advised advised by Prof. Martin Benning and Prof. Carola-Bibiane Sch√∂nlieb. From 2023 to 2025, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Heriot-Watt University and the Maxwell Institute for Mathematical Sciences, where I worked with Prof. Audrey Repetti on unfolded neural networks and plug-and-play algorithms. 

I am currently a postdoctoral research associate in the Institute of Sensors, Signals and Systems at Heriot-Watt University, where I work with Prof. Yoann Altmann on computational imaging, statistical signal processing, and neuromorphic computing. Broadly, I am passionate about developing and integrating ideas from applied mathematics, statistics, and machine learning to create scalable, reliable, and interpretable methods for imaging and signal recovery.
